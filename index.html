University, where I am focused on computationally modeling
complex fluid and thermal systems. Currently, I am working on
developing a state-of-the-art model for predicting the nature of
soot particles emanating from combustion devices. In my free
time, I enjoy traveling, photography, singing, and other
adventurous activities. When I'm not busy with my studies
or hobbies, I also enjoy spending time with
my friends and family.
